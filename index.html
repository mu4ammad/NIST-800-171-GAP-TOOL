About NIST 800-171 Rev. 3

Published May 14, 2024, this revision contains 97 security requirements across 17 families to protect Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) in nonfederal systems and organizations.

Key Changes from Rev. 2: Reduced from 110 to 97 controls, added 3 new control families (Planning, System and Services Acquisition, Supply Chain Risk Management), and introduced organization-defined parameters for flexibility.
